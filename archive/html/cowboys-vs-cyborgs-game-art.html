Cowboys VS Cyborgs

Art for a tower defense game inspired by the movie Cowboys & Aliens, developed as an academic project.

Cowboys VS Cyborgs is a web-based game developed as part of an academic project, inspired by the movie Cowboys & Aliens, and designed to combine classic tower defense mechanics with thematic elements from the film.
This project was developed by a multidisciplinary team of students from Art and Animation, Game Design, and Software Development, aiming to create an engaging gameplay experience that blends sci-fi and Western aesthetics.
