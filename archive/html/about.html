About me

I have a bachelor's degree in Design from UFMA and a master's degree in Design at UFPE. I also studied Computer Games (Art and Animation) for a year at Glasgow Caledonian University (UK).
Since 2014, I have accumulated experience in various fields, having worked in advertising agencies, the public sector, retail companies, and the electric and financial sectors. As a freelancer, I have developed a wide range of projects, mostly focusing on digital products for e-commerce and project management.
