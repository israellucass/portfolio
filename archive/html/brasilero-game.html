Brasilero

2D platform game inspired by the brazilian typeface Brasilêro, developed as an academic project.

Brasilero is a graphic design project developed as part of an academic assignment, inspired by the Brasilêro typeface created by Crystian Cruz. The typeface was designed based on the analysis of hundreds of hand-painted signs found in cities across Brazil, aiming to translate the impact of this popular visual culture into a digital font.
