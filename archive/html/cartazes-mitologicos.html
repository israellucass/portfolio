Myth Posters

Myth Posters is a graphic design project developed during the second semester of a Design degree, as part of the Color and Digital Image course. The assignment consisted of creating a visual piece with an open theme. The final result is a series of minimalist posters inspired by Greco-Roman mythology, featuring graphic elements influenced by literature and the Microsoft PC game Age of Mythology.
